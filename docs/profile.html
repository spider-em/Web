Web: Profile

Display the profile of an image along a row or a column. A display menu allows the setting of different options. The profile can be displayed temporarily or permanently on the screen. The profile can be normalized relative to original image or relative to its row (column). The row or column where the profile is taken is indicated by a line over the image and by its row or column number.
